About

I enjoy learning by building and exploring what AI and human creativity can do.

I'm Seth Grant, a senior at Baylor University's Honors College graduating spring 2026 with degrees in Management Information Systems and Entrepreneurship and Corporate Innovation.

I'm not claiming to be an expert in every tool. I'm someone who enjoys learning by building — exploring what AI tools can do and applying them to real problems. Whether that's a multi-agent n8n workflow for a service business, a LangChain chatbot, a machine learning model, or a Django app for student skill exchange, the through-line is the same: pick up the tool, build something real, and learn from what breaks.

Outside of class, I'm building AuraAnn Skincare, supporting MineralsRx, and working on LymeRevive.org as a public education platform. Top 50 in Baylor's New Venture Competition with AuraAnn was an early validation that the work I want to do can travel.

Faith, family, weightlifting, and self-improvement are the anchors. Curiosity, organization, and a builder's bias are how I move.
